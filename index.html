About myself

Product designer based in Berlin with a technical background as a data analyst. I analyse user needs, extract key insights, and translate them into intuitive, user-friendly solutions. My design approach is grounded in both creativity and analytical thinking.

While studying Human-Computer Interaction, I combined theory with practice in perception psychology, graphic design, and web development. I further developed my UX/UI skills through the SPICED Academy bootcamp. Working as a QA Tester and Data Analyst helped me learn how to translate data into meaningful insights that shape user-focused design.

I believe that good design is essential for a product to succeed.
