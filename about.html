Hi, I'm Mateusz Klos!

Product designer based in Berlin with a technical background as a data analyst. I analyse user needs, extract the most valuable insights, and translate them intouser-friendly solutions.

My journey as a product designer has been a continuous process of gaining diverse experiences. While studying Human-Computer Interaction at Jagiellonian University, I constantly expanded my knowledge by combining theory with practice in areas such as perception psychology, graphic design, and web development. My theoretical knowledge in UX/UI was further deepened through the bootcamp at SPICED Academy, which I successfully completed.

My experience working as a QA Tester and Data Analyst has allowed me to broaden my perspective and create truly user-focused designs. At that time, I gained an understanding of how websites work and how to transform raw data into valuable insights that can help solve a wide range of problems.

I believe that good design is essential for a product to succeed.
